Story

You take a step out of the landing capsule, awestruck.


Beams of moonlight stream through rustling leaves above you like a scene from a movie as the sound of what seems to be crickets permeates the silent night. Fresh air fills your lungs as you take in a deep breath, and the wind gently kisses you on the cheek as it passes. It's been some time since you've set foot on a living planet, let alone one as beautiful as this.


Suddenly - something catches your eye. Beyond a small hill, you see billows of smoke and the glow of a fire warming the horizon. After an arduous trek to the top, you are greeted with an impressive sight.


A huge tower looms over a lush valley of green littered with small groups of people surrounding campfires. People are scurrying about, seeming to be preparing for something, but what?


As you approach the community, a cheery man notices you and gestures you to join his group. Over a crackling campfire, he tells you that their community is made up of explorers from all over the universe, all attracted like flies to this planet. Some come seeking a new life while others come to discover new lands - everyone has their unique backstory. Soon, they'll all be embarking on a great expedition together.


The man remarks that their group still has space for one more for the adventure. With a big grin spread across his face, he turns to you and asks,


"Care to join us?"

ABOUT

At Tembusu College, we believe that this is a Home of Possibilities. The College houses a whole plethora of resources which students can tap upon for freshmen who walk through our doors to kindle passions or break boundaries – a place of self-discovery. We are a community of vastly unique individuals joined by our pursuits of passion and an institution where play and learn coexist in perfect equilibrium.


With this year marking the eleventh year running for Tembusu College, the theme for this year’s Welcome Week is “New Beginnings” in honour of Tembusu’s rich history and culture from the past decade, while still being a place that provides new and boundless possibilities for students to live their best life during their time here.


Therefore, through this Welcome Week, we wish to inspire students to chart their own journey and create their own legacy.
